Tourist Attractions

Hong Kong has a myriad of activities for a variety of visitors. From comfortable family fun to action filled thrills, Hong Kong has something for everyone!

Sight Seeing

Get a glimpse of The Peak, a tram service that takes you 428 meters above sea level while overseeing Hong Kong's tallest skyscrapers.

Shopping

Hong Kong is known for its bustling night markets with street-market type bargins at the Ladies' Market and Temple Street Night Market.

Adventure Parks

Hong Kong is home to Ocean Park, filled with thrill rides and a world-class aquarium ready to keep tourists on their toes.
